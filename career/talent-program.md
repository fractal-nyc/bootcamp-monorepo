## 1. Purpose

Fractal Tech operates an engineering accelerator program and community (“Fractal Tech Talent Program”) made up of current students and alumni (“Talent Members”).

This Agreement outlines the terms under which the Company may hire Talent Members through the Fractal Tech Talent Program and pay a placement fee to Fractal Tech for successful hires.

---

## 2. Qualifying Hires

A **Qualifying Hire** is defined as any Talent Member who:

- Is introduced to the Company through the Fractal Tech Talent Program, and  
- Accepts and begins employment with the Company within **12 months** of introduction.

---

## 3. Success Criteria

A Qualifying Hire is considered **successful** if the Talent Member remains continuously employed by the Company for at least **30 calendar days** after their start date.

If the Talent Member voluntarily resigns or is terminated by the Company for any reason within **45 days** of their start date, Fractal Tech will issue a **full refund** of the placement fee, provided the fee has already been paid.

---

## 4. Placement Fee

For each successful Qualifying Hire, the Company agrees to pay Fractal Tech a **placement fee equal to 15% of the Talent Member’s agreed-upon first-year base salary.**

---

## 5. Payment Terms

- Fractal Tech will issue an invoice on the **Talent Member’s start date.**  
- The Company agrees to pay each invoice within **15 calendar days** of the invoice date.

---

## 6. Non-Exclusivity

This Agreement is **non-exclusive**. The Company is not obligated to hire any Talent Member, and Fractal Tech may offer similar services to other companies.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep confidential any non-public information received from the other in connection with this Agreement and to use such information only for purposes related to this Agreement.

---

## 8. Term and Termination

- This Agreement remains in effect until terminated by either party upon written notice.  
- The Company remains responsible for payment of placement fees for any Qualifying Hires made prior to termination.

---

## 9. Miscellaneous

- This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.  
- This document constitutes the full agreement between the parties on this subject.  
- Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
